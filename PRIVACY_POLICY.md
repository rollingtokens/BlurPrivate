# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date will be updated when published]

## 1. Information We Collect

BlurPrivate is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

- Subscription status and purchase information (handled by Apple)
- App usage preferences and settings (stored locally on your device)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain our services
- To process subscription payments (handled by Apple)
- To improve app functionality and user experience

## 3. Data Storage and Processing

- All photo and video processing is performed locally on your device
- We do not collect, store, or transmit your media content
- User preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults
- Processed works are saved locally using SwiftData

## 4. Photo Library Access

BlurPrivate requires access to your photo library to:

- Select photos and videos for editing
- Save processed media to your library

This access is requested only when needed and can be revoked in iOS Settings at any time.

## 5. Third-Party Services

- We use Apple's StoreKit for subscription management
- Subscription data is handled by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
- We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services

## 6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of data collection (by not using the app)

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
